About Us

Sunlight Computer Academy

Sunlight Computer Academy is a premier Computer Educational Institute offering comprehensive training programs ranging from basic to advanced levels. The academy provides a wide array of professional computer courses and diplomas, including DCA, ADCA, PGDCA, Web Design, Tally with GST, and more. In today's challenging technical world, Sunlight Computer Academy is dedicated to equipping students and communities in rural areas with current market trending technical skills. By doing so, the academy ensures that its students are well-prepared to thrive in the ever-evolving technological landscape.

AAGSU

The All Assam Gorkha Students' Union (AAGSU) is a prestigious organization dedicated to the overall socio-economic development of Gorkhali communities. Known as the backbone of Gorkhali people, AAGSU takes the lead in numerous initiatives aimed at uplifting the community. AAGSU's efforts are focused on various key projects, including the enhancement of the education system for Gorkhali students, promoting games and sports, and fostering cultural and social growth. Through its unwavering commitment, AAGSU continues to be a driving force for progress and unity among Gorkhali people.
