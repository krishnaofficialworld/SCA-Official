Who are We ?

We (a unit of SCA EDUCATIONS) is a team of professionals to lead you towards Technical Expertise through Digital Technology.
Technical Education and its power to drive a generation is undoubtedly one of the most formidable pillar of all the pillar that our society is built upon. Technical education over the years has undergone metamorphosis. SCA EDUCATIONS (SUNLIGHT COMPUTER ACADEMY) strongly believes that the future needs innovative and creative minds with sound Engineering abilities. Team SUNLIGHT COMPUTER ACADEMY strongly believes in imparting robust and industry focused technical knowledge along with leadership qualities that would enable the creation of a better and strong nation, a nation that can hold its head in the community of nations.
